# Advanced Equipment System - User Guide

## Overview

The **Advanced Equipment System** is a fully integrated equipment and inventory management system for Shappa Games. It allows you to equip and manage your character's gear with visual 3D rendering and an intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

## Opening Your Inventory

Press **TAB** on your keyboard to open the inventory panel. Press **TAB** again to close it.

## The Three Panels

When you open your inventory, you'll see three sections:

### Left Panel: Equipment Diagram
A humanoid figure showing your character's body with 7 equipment slots:
- **Head** - Helmets and headgear
- **Torso** - Body armor and vests
- **Left Hand** - Off-hand weapons and shields
- **Right Hand** - Main weapons and tools
- **Back** - Rifles, bows, and backpacks
- **Legs** - Leg armor
- **Feet** - Boots and footwear

Currently equipped items appear as small thumbnails on each slot.

### Center Panel: Inventory Grid
Displays all items you carry. Each item shows:
- **Thumbnail** - Visual representation of the item
- **Name** - Item name

### Right Panel: Item Details
When you click an item, its statistics appear here:
- **Name** - Full item name
- **Type** - Weapon, armor, etc.
- **Rarity** - Common, Uncommon, Rare, etc.
- **Weight** - How much it weighs
- **Damage** (for weapons) - How much damage it deals
- **Defense** (for armor) - How much protection it provides
- **Description** - Detailed information about the item

## Equipping Items

### Method 1: Double-Click
Simply double-click on any item in your inventory to equip it to the recommended slot.

### Method 2: Click + Load Button
1. Click an item to select it (it highlights)
2. Click the **"Load"** button
3. The item equips to its recommended slot

### Method 3: Drag and Drop
1. Click and drag an item from the inventory grid
2. Drop it on an equipment slot in the body diagram
3. The item equips to that slot

## Moving Items Between Slots

You can transfer an item from one slot to another by dragging it:
1. Click and drag an item from one body slot
2. Drop it on a different body slot
3. The item moves to the new position

## Removing Items

To unequip an item and return it to your inventory:
1. Click and drag the item from its equipment slot
2. Drop it somewhere in the center (inventory grid area)
3. The item returns to your inventory

## 3D Visualization

When you equip items, they appear in real-time on your character:
- **Hand items** (pistols, rifles, swords) rotate to face the direction you're looking
- **Armor items** stay on the body as you move
- **Back items** (backpacks, rifles) are visible from behind

## Item Types

### Weapons
- **Damage**: Increases attack power
- **Weight**: How cumbersome the weapon is
- Example: Beretta 92FS, M1911, Fragmentation Grenade

### Armor
- **Defense**: Reduces incoming damage
- **Weight**: Affects movement speed
- Example: Tactical Helmet, Kevlar Vest, Combat Boots

### Accessories
- **Special Effects**: Varies by item
- **Capacity**: Storage space (for backpacks)
- Example: Tactical Backpack

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Weight Management** - Lighter gear allows faster movement. Balance protection with mobility.

2. **Slot Suggestions** - If an item can go in multiple slots, it automatically suggests the "primary" slot, but you can put it anywhere.

3. **Quick Reload** - If you pick up a new weapon and want to equip it fast: Double-click it.

4. **Organize Your Inventory** - Keep frequently used items at the top of your inventory grid for easy access.

5. **Check Stats** - Always click items to see their full stats before equipping, especially if you're deciding between similar items.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I see my equipped items outside of the inventory panel?**  
A: Yes! Equipped items render on your character in the game world. You can see hand-held weapons from your first-person view, and other players (if multiplayer) can see your full equipment.

**Q: What happens to unequipped items?**  
A: They stay in your inventory grid until you use them, sell them, or drop them.

**Q: Can I carry unlimited items?**  
A: No. Your inventory has a maximum capacity. Once full, you must drop or use items to make room.

**Q: Are there cooldowns on equipping items?**  
A: No. You can equip and unequip items instantly. There's no penalty for switching gear frequently.

**Q: Can I customize where items appear on my body?**  
A: In the current version, item positions are fixed per slot type. Administrators can customize positions in the game settings file.

**Q: Do equipped items affect my gameplay?**  
A: Yes! Armor provides defense bonuses, weapons add damage, and weight affects movement speed. Always consider the tradeoffs.
